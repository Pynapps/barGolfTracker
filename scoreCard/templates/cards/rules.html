Rules

How to Play


Overview

The goal of Bar Golf is to have the lowest score out of all the players. The way to do that is to DRINK!!!

Each type of drink (beer, mixed drink, shot, etc.) has a value associated with it. There are also penalties for certain actions, like throwing up or drinking water, that will hurt your score.

You will record the total of all of your drinks/penalties at a particular bar/hole in "Your Card".

Remember to Save your card after each bar/hole so you don't lose or forget your progress.

The par value is the desired score for that bar/hole. Don't feel like you need to hit this score, it's just a goal.

Lastly, have fun and be safe! This is all in good fun, so don't feel like you need to drink more than you can handle.

Scoring

Each type of drink has a value associated with it. The values are as follows:

Beer: -1
Wine: -2
Mixed Drinks: -2
Bomb Shots: -3
Shots: -3


There are also penalties for certain actions. The penalties are as follows:

Drinking a Water: +1
Bathroom Break: +2
Throwing Up: +5


If you don't know the value of a drink, ask someone who can make that call or use your best judgement.

The total score for a bar/hole is the sum of all your drinks and penalties.
